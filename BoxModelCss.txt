CSS Box Model :
According to the Box Model , Each and every element on a webPage Can be Seen as a rectangular Box .  Each Box will have a margin , padding , width , height and border . 
- [ ] Content : text , images etc .
- [ ] Padding : Transparent Area around the content , inside of the Box .
- [ ] Border : Goes Around the padding and the content ;
- [ ] Margin : Space Between Boxes ;
- [ ] Fill Area : Area That gets filled with background color and background images .

Block elements are the elements which create their own  box while inline elements does not .
